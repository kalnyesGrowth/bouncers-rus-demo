FAQ

Common Questions
Before You Book

How far in advance should I book? +
We recommend booking at least 1–2 weeks in advance, especially for weekends and summer months. That said, we do accommodate last-minute and same-day bookings when availability allows — just call or text us and we'll do our best!
Do I need to pay a deposit? +
No deposit required to get a quote or to hold your date in most cases. We'll work with you on payment — typically collected at delivery. Call us for details on your specific booking.
Is delivery, setup, and takedown included? +
Yes! Delivery, setup, and takedown are included with every rental. We bring everything to your event location, set it up safely, and return to pack it all up when the party is over. You don't have to lift a finger.
What happens if it rains on my event day? +
Safety comes first. If there's rain, high winds, or lightning, we'll work with you to reschedule. We understand that Texas weather is unpredictable and we'll always find a solution that works for your family. Contact us as early as possible if weather is a concern.
How clean is the equipment? +
Every piece of equipment is thoroughly cleaned and sanitized between every single rental. We take hygiene seriously — especially for inflatables that kids are in contact with. Our equipment is also regularly inspected for safety.
How much space do I need for a bounce house? +
Most standard bounce castles need approximately 15x15 feet of flat, clear space. Water slides require more room — typically 15x25 feet or more depending on the unit. We'll confirm exact dimensions when you book and can advise you on setup for your specific yard or venue.
Do you serve events outside of San Antonio? +
Yes! We regularly deliver to Live Oak, Universal City, Converse, Schertz, Selma, Helotes, New Braunfels, and many surrounding communities. If you're outside our standard area, call us — we may still be able to help depending on the date and event.
¿Hablan español? / Do you speak Spanish? +
¡Sí! We're fully bilingual — English and Spanish. Feel free to call or text us in whichever language is most comfortable for you. We serve San Antonio's entire community and want every family to feel at home.
